The Midnight Story

Midnight's Woodworking was born from a love of craftsmanship and the cherished memory of Midnight, a loyal companion who brought joy and inspiration to every day. Located in the heart of Buffalo, NY, we combine traditional woodworking techniques with modern resin artistry to create unique, functional pieces that tell a story.

Every piece is handcrafted with care using sustainably sourced hardwoods and high-quality epoxy resins. We take pride in our attention to detail, from the initial design concept to the final food-safe finish. Whether you're looking for Buffalo Bills-inspired home décor or a custom wedding gift, each creation carries the spirit of craftsmanship and the warmth of Western New York hospitality.

Our commitment to quality means using only food-safe finishes on cutting boards and serving pieces, heat-resistant resins for coasters, and durable construction that will last for years to come. We believe in creating heirloom-quality pieces that can be passed down through generations, just like the memories of those we hold dear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are resin coasters heat-resistant?

Yes—our coasters handle typical mug temperatures; avoid direct contact with cookware straight from the stove/oven.

What finishes do you use?

Food-safe oils/waxes for boards and durable resin topcoats for coasters/holders.

Do you accept custom orders?

Absolutely. Choose colors, monograms, dates, or theme inspiration. Typical lead time: 2 weeks.

How do I care for my wood and resin pieces?

Hand wash with mild soap and warm water. Avoid dishwashers and prolonged soaking. Dry immediately after washing.

Do you ship outside of Buffalo?

Yes! We ship nationwide. All items are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery.

What's your return policy?

We stand behind our quality. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of delivery for a resolution.
